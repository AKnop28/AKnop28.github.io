Entrepreneurship

During my Sophomore year of college I came across the problem of not being able to charge a phone or have a fan up in a lofted bed. I designed and created what I call the "Golden Arm" to solve my problem. After taking several business classes I began to think of making a business out of it. During the summer of 2014, my Dad and I built 14 Golden Arms to sell to interested customers at Luther College. I have sold all 14 and have people asking for more. It's been a crazy experience, filled with a lot of learning opportunities that include advertising to other people and creatively selling a product in our social media dominated culture today.
